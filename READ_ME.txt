**Open StevenWoodheadGradeUnit.sln**

**In the Solution Explorer
	Open Database Folder
	Double Click on LocalDB.mdf

**In Server Explorer
	Right Click on LocalDB
	Open Properties.
	**In Properties
		Copy the "Connection String" 

**In Solution Explorer
	Open Properties Folder.
	Double Click on Settings.settings.
	Delete the information in Value.
	Paste "Connection String" into Value.

	**Important**
	Still in Settings.settings
	Top Left - Hit Synchronize

Program Will now run in Visual Studio.
